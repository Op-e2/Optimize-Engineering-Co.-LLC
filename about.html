About Optimize Engineering Co. LLC

Licensed Professional Consulting Engineer providing trusted solar, structural, and mechanical engineering since 2001.

Who We Are

Founded in 2001 and based in Farmville, Virginia, Optimize Engineering Co. LLC is a licensed professional engineering and consulting firm specializing in renewable energy and structural design. Our team provides solar energy system design, roof and foundation analysis, and mechanical and electrical engineering support for installers, developers, and building owners.

Licensed Professional Consulting Engineer

As a licensed professional engineering firm, Optimize Engineering delivers plan sets, design calculations, and peer reviews for solar and structural projects across multiple states. We provide code-compliant, review-ready engineering documents for both residential and commercial applications — ensuring safety, reliability, and permitting success. Our consulting services include roof framing verification, array support design, electrical schematic review, and custom mechanical calculations.

Professional Licensure

Optimize Engineering Co. LLC holds active Professional Engineer (P.E.) licenses in the following jurisdictions:

SD, NJ, NY, PA, ME, VA, NH, CT, MA, MD, NC, TN, RI, HI, SC, DC, WV

If you need services outside these areas, contact us to discuss partnership options.

Our Approach

  • Designs are concise, constructible, and tailored to local jurisdiction requirements.
  • Structural and electrical calculations meet current building codes and NEC standards.
  • Submittals are review-friendly and minimize delays in approval and permitting.
  • Every project receives professional oversight from a licensed engineer.
Clear, thorough submittals reduce rework, accelerate approvals, and keep projects on schedule.
