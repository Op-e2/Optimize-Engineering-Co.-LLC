What we do

Engineering Services

Permit-ready plans, calculations, and professional reviews for safe, code-compliant installations.

Solar PV – Structural

Stamped structural calculations and drawings for roof-mounted and ground-mounted arrays.

  • Framing analysis (rafters, trusses, purlins)
  • Attachment layout and spacing
  • Footings, piers, ballast (for ground systems)
  • Wind & snow per ASCE 7 and local amendments

Solar PV – Electrical

NEC-based review of one-lines, conductor sizing, OCPDs, rapid shutdown, and interconnection.

  • Module/string & inverter selection checks
  • Conductor ampacity, derating, & voltage drop
  • Busbar calculations & supply-side taps
  • Labels and rapid-shutdown compliance

Observatory Domes

Specialty calculations for new dome installations and retrofits.

  • Support ring & anchorage design
  • Horizontal/vertical reactions & deflection
  • Connection details & fastener schedules

Mechanical Engineering

General mechanical engineering services including equipment supports and custom components.

  • Support frames and platforms
  • Brackets, guards, and housings
  • Fabrication-ready drawings
